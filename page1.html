Note to Parents: The Duck story is meant to be read on a desktop computer or laptop. It's a great way to help kids learn how to interface with computers. Most children grow up with an ability to navigate mobile devices better than they can a mouse and keyboard. Consider making this an activity that you can do with your child! The story encourages engagement between kids and parents.
